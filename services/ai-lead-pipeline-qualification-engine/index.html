The Problem

Good leads arrive, then go cold

Slow first response

Qualified prospects wait too long, then book with whoever replies first.

Noisy inbox

Bots, spam, and low-fit inquiries consume the same effort as high-intent leads.

No priority model

Teams treat every inquiry equally instead of routing by intent and value.

System Blueprint

Capture -> Guardrails -> Score -> Route

A modular intake architecture that blocks junk, classifies intent, and pushes each lead to the right action path.

01

Capture

Collect every inquiry through secure webhook intake with normalized payloads and baseline anti-spam controls.

02

Guardrails

Fail-closed validation blocks malformed or risky payloads before they reach your CRM or team inbox.

03

Score

AI plus deterministic fallback rules classify leads into hot, warm, cold, and spam with predictable behavior.

04

Route

High-intent leads trigger instant alerts, lower-intent paths get nurtured, and all activity is logged for ops.

Fit + Outcomes

Who this system is for and what changes first

Use this system when these conditions are true, and skip it when they are not.

Good fit

  • Teams with meaningful inbound volume and inconsistent response speed
  • Sales pipelines where lead quality varies sharply across channels
  • Founders who want client-owned automation, not a black-box vendor flow

Probably not a fit

  • Businesses with near-zero inbound and no outreach strategy
  • Teams unwilling to define qualification criteria
  • Setups that require unreviewed full-autonomous outbound messaging
