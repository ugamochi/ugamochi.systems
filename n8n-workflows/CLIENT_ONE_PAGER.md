# Lead Automation System - Client One Pager

## What It Does
This system captures website leads, filters spam, scores lead quality, logs every submission, and sends the right follow-up automatically.

## What You Get
- Instant lead capture from your site form
- Internal notification email with structured lead details
- Automatic client reply tailored by lead quality (`hot`, `warm`, `cold`)
- Security guardrails to block obvious bot/spam submissions
- Google Sheets lead log as a simple CRM layer
- Optional instant hot-lead alert to Slack/Telegram/webhook

## Why It Matters
- Faster replies increase conversion chance
- Sales time goes to real leads, not manual triage
- Clear scoring improves follow-up priority
- Fewer spam interruptions in your inbox
- Simple daily operations for a small team

## Delivery Timeline
- Build and connect: 1-2 days
- Testing and tuning: 1 day
- Go-live and handover: same week

## What We Need From You
- n8n workspace access
- Email account connection (Gmail/SMTP)
- Google Sheet for lead logging
- Website form endpoint details
- Optional alert destination (Slack/Telegram)

## Security and Ownership
- You keep full ownership of accounts and billing
- Credentials stay in your n8n instance
- No long-term shared passwords
- Handover includes runbook + rollback steps

## Success Criteria
- Valid leads are delivered, scored, and logged
- Spam is blocked or downgraded
- Hot leads trigger fast action
- Team can run the system without vendor lock-in
